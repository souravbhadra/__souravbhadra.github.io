About Me

I am passionate about applying geospatial data science, remote sensing, unmanned aerial vehicles and machine learning in answering critical issues related to agriculture, food security, and nautal resources dynamics. My goal is to combine the knowledge of physical remote sensing and data-driven machine learning techniques, so there can be robust applicability or reproducibility of the model worldwide. I typically use Python-based geospatial and data science packages to analyze, model and visualize different sets of data. I am also keen in understaning the dynamics of natural resources (land cover, water streams and other soil quality) through spatial analysis. In short, if there is remotely sensed images, geospatial data, and an ocean of unanswered questions, I am ready to dive in...
